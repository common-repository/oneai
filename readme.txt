Description
-----------
The OneAI plugin allows you to easily integrate your OneAI Agent into your WordPress site.

3rd party
----------
The plugin requires a subscription to oneai.com (free subscription is available)
Subscription information: https://oneai.com/agent/pricing
Terms of Service: https://oneai.com/tos
Privacy Policy: https://oneai.com/privacy-policy

Installation
------------
1.  **Create Your Agent:**
    
    *   Go to [OneAI](https://oneai.com/~agentmaker).
    *   Upload the URL of your website to create an agent.
    *   Once the agent is created, click the 'share' button and copy the agent's URL.
2.  **Upload Plugin:**
    
    *   Upload the plugin folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ folder.
    *   Go to the **Plugins** page and activate the plugin.
3.  **Configure Settings:**
    
    *   Go to the 'OneAI' settings page in your admin panel.
    *   Enter your agent's URL and save the settings.

Usage
-----

After installation and activation, your agent will be automatically added into your site.

Customization
-------------

To modify the appearance and behavior of your agent, go to the [Agent Studio](https://studio.oneai.com/agent) and make your changes. All changes will be automatically reflected on your site.
